About MuktoHoste:~

  • MuktoHoste was established entirely with public contributions and does not get any budgetary support.
  • MuktoHoste accepts voluntary contributions from Individuals, Organizations, Trusts, Companies and Institutions etc.
  • All contributions towards MuktoHoste are exempt from Income Tax.
  • The resources of the MuktoHoste are utilized to render immediate relief to families of those killed in natural calamities like COVID-19, floods, cyclones and Dought, etc.
  • Assists partially to defray the expenses for medical treatment like heart surgery, kidney transplantation, cancer treatment of needy people and acid attack etc.
  • Donations are accepted through BHIM/UPI, Credit Cards / Debit Cards / Netbanking Accounts of Indian Banks only.
